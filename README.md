## Project Objective
The Brain Pharmaceutical Store management needs insights into sales performance, product demand, regional trends, and revenue drivers to support better business decisions.

- Which products generate the highest total sales?
- Which country performs best in terms of sales?
- How does sales performance change over time (yearly sales trend)?
- What are the top and bottom performing products?
- What is the total sales value across the dataset?

## key Insights 
- The total revenue across the dataset is $1,711,583.00, representing the cumulative performance across all distributors.
- Ribabyclor emerged as the highest revenue generator with sales of $240,597.00, contributing significantly to the overall margin.
- Poland is currently the top-performing market, with total sales of Poland is $1,140,558.00, making 67% of the total sales. This suggests a strong distributor network or high demand in this specific geography.
- Analysis shows a Steady Growth in yearly sales, specifically in 201,8 which may correlate with pharmaceutical demand cycles.
- The bottom-performing products were identified as Afluferon Entrarenone, Aggrakine, Cricane, Acamsumab, Immurenol Temarate, and Raparidol, with Immurenol Temarate being the least performing product. These items may require a review of marketing strategy or potential discontinuation.
## Final Conclusion
The analysis successfully moved from a raw data state to a structured insights framework. The findings highlight a strong concentration of revenue in specific products and regions, while also identifying "blind spots" where sales are lagging.
